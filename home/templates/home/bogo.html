BOGOMOLETS NATIONAL MEDICAL UNIVERSITY

It was founded in the year 1841. The university is named after physiologist Alexander A. Bogomolets. The university provides medical training for over 10,000 students, including about 1,300 foreigners from 56 countries. It is one of the best medical universities in Ukraine. Among its 1,500 teaching staff, 855 are PhD and 205 Doctors of Sciences. So the education quality is topmost in this University

Duration

Mbbs course duration is 5.8 years which also includes the internship. In all medical universities in Ukraine, course duration for MBBS is 5.8 years.

Eligibility

  • Student must complete their Secondary education with minimum 45% marks in PCB.
  • Minimum age to apply is 17 years by 31st December of the admission year
  • Must have qualified NEET exam
  • Only regular student can apply

Courses

  • General Medicine (MBBS)
  • Preventive Medicine
  • Paediatrics
  • Dentistry
  • Higher Nursing Education

About us

Students in India preparing for NEET or AIIMS couldn't continue their dream of becoming a MBBS doctor because of lesser seats in government medical universities and very expensive college fees in private colleges. As a results students either keeps appearing for NEET or switches their stream from MBBS to other medical courses like nursing, dentistry, etc. We at Admissions in Ukraine provide an opportunity to students of pursuing their dreams of becoming a doctor. Doing MBBS in Ukraine is very cheaper as compared to the private medical colleges in India. So, we guide the students with one of our possible solution to make a carrier in MBBS. Our journey started in the year 2019 where we sent some students from India to Ivano-Frankvisk. Learning from that experience this year we are trying to make more students dream come true. One shouldn't drop the idea of becoming a doctor because of his/her incapability of paying high college fees. If you have qualified NEET and got admitted in a government college then its the best, else give us an opportunity to open new paths to you.

Your Benefits

College Ranking

All of our selected universities are very reputed one. They are old and well established. All of them are ranked among top universities of Ukraine.

International Exposure

You will get a chance to spend 6 years of your life in a foreign country. Learning a new language and experience a different culture would be very wonderful.

Low-Budget MBBS

Indian Community

DO YOU HAVE ANY QUESTIONS?

These are the most common questions that arise in a students mind.

Is there any exam we need to qualify before getting an admission?

  • NEET Qualified
  • 12 Marksheet (Minimum 45% in PCB)
  • No other entrance exam

Is Ukraine safe for Indian students?

  • Yes, it is very safe for Indian students.

Availability of Indian food in Ukraine?

  • There are certain mess and restaurant that specifically provides Indian food.

How is the climate in Ukraine?

  • If you love cold places, Ukraine is for you.
  • You will experience snowfall here in winters
  • While in the summer you will find it greener like nowhere else.

What is included in the 30 lakhs budget?

  • All your document verification, clearance and translation.
  • Invitation Letter
  • 6 years VISA
  • 6 years Tuition fee
  • 6 years Hostel accommodation
  • 6 years Mess charges
  • 6 years Health Insurance

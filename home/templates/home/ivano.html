IVANO-FRANKIVSK NATIONAL MEDICAL UNIVERSITY

The Ivano-Frankivsk National Medical University is situated in the Ivano city of Ukraine. It was established in the year 1945. The university consists of its 12 hospitals and clinics. The IFNMU is a member of

  • European Association of Universities
  • World Health Organization
  • Other International Organizations

It contains 48 departments and in the year 2009 IFNMU Library received the prize for "Library of the year" in the All Ukrainian Contest. It is one of those handful of Medical Universities in Ukraine which has been designated as a “National Medical University” by the legislature of Ukraine.

Duration

Mbbs course duration is 5.8 years which also includes the internship. In all medical universities in Ukraine, course duration for MBBS is 5.8 years.

Eligibility

  • Student must complete their Secondary education with minimum 45% marks in PCB.
  • Minimum age to apply is 17 years by 31st December of the admission year
  • Must have qualified NEET exam
  • Only regular student can apply

Courses

  • General Medicine (MBBS)
  • Preventive Medicine
  • Paediatrics
  • Dentistry
  • Higher Nursing Education

LVIV NATIONAL MEDICAL UNIVERSITY

LNMU is situated in the beautiful city of Lviv. It is one of the most beautiful cities of Ukraine. The university was established in the year 1784. Every year it ranks amoung top 3 Medical universities of the Country. In 2009, when the medical university celebrated its 225th anniversary since its foundation the Government of Ukraine launched a 5 Ukrainian Hryvnia Coin with the Seal, Name and Motto of the Medical University in the Honour and Respect of the Prestigious Institution.

Duration

Mbbs course duration is 5.8 years which also includes the internship. In all medical universities in Ukraine, course duration for MBBS is 5.8 years.

Eligibility

  • Student must complete their Secondary education with minimum 45% marks in PCB.
  • Minimum age to apply is 17 years by 31st December of the admission year
  • Must have qualified NEET exam
  • Only regular student can apply

Courses

  • General Medicine (MBBS)
  • Preventive Medicine
  • Paediatrics
  • Dentistry
  • Higher Nursing Education

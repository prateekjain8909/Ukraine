POLTAVA STATE MEDICAL UNIVERSITY

The Poltava State Medical University is situated in the Poltava city of Ukraine. It was established in the year 1921. The university occupies 16 buildings including

  • 5 academic buildings
  • 4 dormitories
  • Apartments
  • Vivarium
  • Library
  • Sports Complex
  • Dining room
  • Cafe

PSMNU is recognized by

  • European Association of Universities
  • World Health Organization (WHO)
  • I-MED School
  • Medical Council of India (MCI)
  • Medical Council of Nepal
  • PMDC

As of 2004, PSMU had a level-IV accreditation

Duration

Mbbs course duration is 5.8 years which also includes the internship. In all medical universities in Ukraine, course duration for MBBS is 5.8 years.

Eligibility

  • Student must complete their Secondary education with minimum 45% marks in PCB.
  • Minimum age to apply is 17 years by 31st December of the admission year
  • Must have qualified NEET exam
  • Only regular student can apply

Courses

  • General Medicine (MBBS)
  • Preventive Medicine
  • Paediatrics
  • Dentistry
  • Higher Nursing Education
